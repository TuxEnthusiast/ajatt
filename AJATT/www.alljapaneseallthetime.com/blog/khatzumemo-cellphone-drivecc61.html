KhatzuMemo Cellphone Drive

by khatzumoto

Friends, Romans, countrymen. I’ve had isolated reports from some people of KhatzuMemo not working on their phones. This is serious, not least because KhatzuMemo is all about…working on people’s phones.

So for the purposes of helping everyone out, if you use, have used, or have tried to use KhatzuMemo on your cellphone, please, by way of comment on this post, let everyone know the [provider], [make and model] of the cellphone you use. Here is an example of how you might do it.

[Softbank][Sharp 904SH][OK]

In the case that KhatzuMemo doesn’t work on your cellphone, please feel free to share that information, too. Like so:

[Provider][Make and model][NG][symptoms, e.g. "login page fine, but afterwards blank"]

“OK” is for if it works. “NG” (no good) for if it doesn’t. If it doesn’t work, describe, if you can, what happens.

[Update: This problem has been fixed].
